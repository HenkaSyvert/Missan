Member Enumeration Documentation

◆ Projection

enum class Missan::Camera::Projection
strong

The types of projection supported by the Camera class. Not all fields are used by all projections.

Enumerator
Orthographic 

In an orthogonal projection, all projection lines are orthogonal to the projection plane (thence the name). Orthographic projection uses orthographicSize, aspectRatio, and the Camera's Transform.

Perspective 

A perspective projection gives the standard 3D effect of objects further away being smaller. Perspective projection uses fieldOfView, near- and farClipPlane, aspectRatio, and the Camera's Transform.
